Our Story

Rooted in Ancient Tradition

Secretos Del Sur is a catering project rooted in the culinary traditions of the Yucatán peninsula — a cuisine born from the ancient Maya civilization and carried through generations. From the sacred B'en glyph that marks our identity to the flavors that define our menus, everything we do is an expression of cultural pride brought to your table.

From Yucatán to Amarillo, Texas — Martha Cabrera founded Secretos Del Sur with one mission: to bring authentic Yucatecan flavors to the events that matter most. Every display, every bar, every detail is crafted with the elegance and warmth that only a deep cultural connection can produce.

The B'en Symbol

Our logo is the Maya B'en glyph — meaning Junco, the Reed. In the Maya Tzolkin calendar, B'en represents the growth of grain and abundance. It is the foundation of our identity.
